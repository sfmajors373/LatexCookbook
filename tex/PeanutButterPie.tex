{Peanut Butter Pie}

    %\introduction{

    \ingredients{%
        2 & Graham Cracker Crusts \\
	8 oz & Cream Cheese \\
	1 1/2 cup & Powdered Sugar \\
	2 tubs & Cool Whip \\
	1 cup & Peanut Butter \\
	& Hershey Syrup \\
	& Mini Reese Cups \\
    }
    
    \preparation{%
        \step Cream cream cheese.  Beat in powdered sugar.  Add cool whip and peanut butter.  Mix well.
	\step Fold in reese's.
	\step Drizzle syrup into shells.  Divide filling into the two crusts.  Top with additional syrup and mini Reese cups.
    }
