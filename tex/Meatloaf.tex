	2 Tbsp & Seasoning Salt \\
	1 1/2 lbs & Lean Ground Beef \\
	1 & Egg \\
	1 cup & Sharp Cheddar Cheese, divided \\
	 & Chopped Cilantro \\
    }
    
    \preparation{%
        \step Chop pepper and onion.  Mix all ingredients and place in baking dish.
	\step Bake.  Can be basted with additional barbeque sauce, if desired.
	\step Top with cheese and cilantro.
    }
