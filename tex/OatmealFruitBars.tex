{Oatmeal Fruit Bars}

        small=pic/glass,     % small picture
        big=pic/ingredients  % big picture
    }
    
    \introduction{
    A quick recipe to take for a last minute get-together.  The grocery store has many good fruit filling options in the baked goods department.
    }
    
    \ingredients{%
        2 pkg & Yellow Cake Mix \\
        2 1/2 cups & Quick Cooking Oats\\
        3/4 cup & Softened Butter\\
        1 & Egg\\
        2 jars & Baker's filling OR\\
        1/2 - 3/4 tube & Fruit Pastry Filling\\
    }
    
    \preparation{%
        \step Preheat oven and grease 9x13 pan.
        \step Combine cake mix and oats.
        \step Add butter and eggs.
        \step Press half of mixture in bottom of pan.
        \step Spread fruit filling on top.
        \step Sprinkle remaining mixture over the fruit filling.
        \step Bake for 22 - 26 minutes.
    }
