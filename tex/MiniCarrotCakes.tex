{Mini Carrot Cakes}
    
    \graph
    {% pictures
        small=pic/glass,     % small picture
        big=pic/ingredients  % big picture
    }
    
    %\introduction{

    %}
    
    \ingredients{%
        1 9oz pkg & Yellow Cake Mix \\
	1 1/2 tsp & Allspice \\
        1 & Egg \\
        2 Tbls & Vegetable Oil \\
        3/4 cup & Grated Carrot \\
        Filling: \\
        6 oz & Cream Cheese \\
        3/4 cup & Powdered Sugar \\
        1/2 cup & Thawed Whipped Topping \\
    }
    
    \preparation{%
        \step Combine cake mix, allspice, egg and oil until ingredients are moistened.  Batter will be thick.  Stir in grated carrots.
	\step Grease mini muffin pan and fill each cup 2/3 full.
	\step Bake for 11 minutes.  Press the tops of the cakes with a mini-tart shaper and allow to cool.
	\step Mix softened cream cheese and sugar until smooth.  Fold in whipped topping.
	\step Spoon filling into piping bag (or gallon sized ziplock bag) and pipe on top of the little cakes.
    }
