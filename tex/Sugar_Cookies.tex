{Sugar Cookies }
    
    \graph
    {% pictures
        small=pic/glass,     % small picture
        big=pic/ingredients  % big picture
    }
    
    \introduction{
        Fun cookies to make with children: very easy, and tolerant of the extra love from them.  Not overly sweet, so they are good for icing.
    }
    
    \ingredients{%
        3 cups & Flour\\
        1/2 tsp & Baking Powder\\
        1/2 tsp & Baking Soda\\
        1 cup & Cold Butter\\
        2 & Eggs\\
        1 cup & Sugar
        }
    
    \preparation{%
        \step Using a pastry knife, cut the butter into the flour, baking soda and baking powder.
        \step In a separate vessel, mix the sugar and the eggs.  Combine the crumb mixture with the egg mixture and mix well.
        \step Roll out, cut and bake for 5 to 6 minutes.
        \step Allow to cool and decorate to suit your fancy.
    }
