{Orange Cake}

    {% pictures
        small=pic/glass,     % small picture
        big=pic/ingredients  % big picture
    }
    
    \introduction{
    A very moist and flavorful cake
    }
    
    \ingredients{%
        4 & Eggs \\
        3/4 cup & Water\\
        3/4 cup & Oil\\
        1 pkg & Yellow Cake Mix\\
        1 small box & Lemon Pudding\\
        Glaze:\\
        2 cups & Powdered Sugar\\
        2 Tbsp & Melted Butter\\
        2 Tbsp & Hot Water\\
        3/4 cup & Orange Juice\\
        1 tsp & Vanilla\\
    }    
    \preparation{%
        \step Mix all cake ingredients together and pour into a greased 9x13 pan.
        \step Bake for 35 - 40 minutes.
        \step Let cake cool and once cool, poke holes in cake with fork.
        \step Combine all glaze ingredients in a saucepan; stir.
        \step Let glaze come to a boil.  Remove from the stove and pour over cake.
        \step Before serving sprinkle with powdered sugar.

    }
