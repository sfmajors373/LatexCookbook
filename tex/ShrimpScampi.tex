{Shrimp Scampi}
    
    \graph

    %\introduction{

    %}
    
    \ingredients{%
        8 oz & Uncooked Pasta \\
	1 Tbls & Olive Oil \\
	2 lbs & Large Shrimp, Peeled and Deveined \\
	1 Tbls & Minced Garlic \\
	1/4 cup & Chopped Green Onions \\
	2 Tbls & Lemon Juice \\
	2 Tbls & Brandy or Sherry, optionial \\
	1/4 cup & Chopped Parsley \\
	1/4 tsp & Salt \\
	4 Tbls & Butter \\
    }
    
    \preparation{%
        \step Fill large pot 3/4 full with water and bring to a boil.  Add pasta and cook according to package directions.  Drain thoroughly.
	\step while pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.  Add the shrimp and cook for about 3 minutes.  Turn the shrimp and cook until pink and opaque, about 2 minutes longer.  Transfer to a bowl and keep warm.
	\step Add garlic and green onions to saucepan and cook until fragrant, about 10 seconds.  Add lemon juice, brandy/sherry, parsley, salt, and pepper.  Remove the saucepan from the heat and add butter and cooked shrimp.  Toss until shrimp are coated with sauce.
	\step Divide pasta among bowls and top with shrimp and sauce.
    }
