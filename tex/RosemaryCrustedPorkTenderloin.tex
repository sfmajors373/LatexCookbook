{Rosemary-Crusted Pork Tenderloin with Vegetables}
    
    \graph
    {% pictures
        small=pic/glass,     % small picture

    }
    
    %\introduction{

    %}
    
    \ingredients{%
	2 (1 lb each) & Pork Tenderloins \\
	1 clove & garlic \\
	2 Tbls & Rosemary \\
	1/2 tsp & Salt \\
	Vegetables: \\
	1 1/2 lbs & Small Red Potatoes \\
	1 & Red Bell Pepper, cut in 1" pieces \\
	1 & Onion, cut in 1/4" wedges \\
	1 Tbls & Olive Oil \\
	2 cloves & Garlic \\
	1 tsp & Rosemary \\
	1/2 tsp & Salt \\
	1/4 tsp & Ground Pepper \\
    }
    
    \preparation{%
        \step Place tenderloins side by side but not touching on bar pan.  Rub surface of pork with pressed garlic.
	\step In small bowl, combine rosemary and salt.  Rub over pork.
	\step Mix Vegetables and seasonings.  Place vegetables surrounding pork.
	\step Bake for 25 minutes.  Remove from oven and let stand for 5 minutes before serving.
    }
