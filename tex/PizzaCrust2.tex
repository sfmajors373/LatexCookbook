{Pizza Crust}
    
    \graph
    {% pictures
        small=pic/glass,     % small picture
        big=pic/ingredients  % big picture
    }
    
    \introduction{
This is not a thin crust pizza, but is not as fluffy as the alternative pizza crust.  Also it makes two pizzas.
    }
    
    \ingredients{%
        2 cups & Water \\
        1 Tbls & Sugar \\
        1 Tbls & Salt \\
        1 pkg & Yeast \\
        2 Tbls & Olive Oil \\
        5 - 6 Cups & Flour \\
        & Flavorings
    }
    
    \preparation{%
        \step Mix together the ingredients, and knead.
        \step Let raise.
        \step Divide in half.  Pat each half into a circle and add toppings.
        \step Bake until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
    }
    
    %\suggestion[Finishing]
    %{%
        
    %}
    
    \suggestion{%
If you do not need both pizzas at the moment, after allowing to raise, freeze half.  When you need it, thaw, top, and bake.
    }
