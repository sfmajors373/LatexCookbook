{Pot Pie}
    
    \graph
    {% pictures

    %\introduction{

    %}
    
    \ingredients{%
        1/3 cup & Butter \\
        1/3 cup & Chopped Onion \\
        1/3 cup & Flour \\
        14 oz & Broth \\
        1/2 cup & Milk \\
        2 cups & Vegetables \\
        & Chicken
    }
    
    \preparation{%
        \step Saute the onions in the butter, then add the flour.  Add the broth and milk and cook until thick.
        \step Stir in the vegetables and chicken.
        \step Pour into pie crust and bake.
    }
