{Spaghetti with Shrimp and Arugula}
    
    \graph
    {% pictures
        small=pic/glass,     % small picture

    }
    
    %\introduction{

    %}
    
    \ingredients{%
        1 lb & Dry Spaghetti \\
	2 cloves & Garlic \\
	1 - 2 & Dried Red Chilis, Crumbled \\
	1 lb & Peeled Raw Large Shrimp \\
	1 small wineglass & White Wine \\
	2 heaped Tbls & sundried tomato puree \\
	1 & Lemon, Zest and Juice \\
	2 handfuls & Arugula, Roughly Chopped \\
    }
    
    \preparation{%
        \step Cook spaghetti in large pot of salted boiling water according to the packet instructions.
	\step Heat 3 'good lugs' of extra virgin olive oil in large frying pan and toss in the garlic and chili.  As the garlic begins to color add shrimp.  Saute them for a minute.
	\step Add white wine and the tomato puree and simmer for a coupe minutes.
	\step When pasta is ready, drain it, reserving a little of the cooking water.
	\step Toss spaghetti with the sauce, squeeze in the lemon juice , add half the chopped arugula, adding a little of the reserved cooking water if you want to loosen the sauce a bit.
	\step Divide pasta and sprnkle with grated lemon zest and the rest of the arugula leaves.
    }
