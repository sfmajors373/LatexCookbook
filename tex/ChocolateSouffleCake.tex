{Chocolate Souffle Cake}
    
    \graph
    {% pictures
        small=pic/glass,     % small picture
        big=pic/ingredients  % big picture
    }
    
    %\introduction{

    %}
    
    \ingredients{%
        3 Tbls & Sugar \\
	1 Tbls & Butter \\
	4 oz & Bittersweet Chocolate, chopped \\
	2 & Eggs, separated \\
	1/8 tsp & Cinnamon \\
    }
    
    \preparation{%
	\step Spray 2 prep bowls (coffee mug sized bowls) and coat with sugar.  
	\step Melt butter and chocolate and set aside to cool.
	\step Beat egg whites until foamy.  Continue beating, gradually adding sugar and beat until soft peaks form.
	\step Whisk egg yolks and cinnamon into cooled chocolate mixture.  Fold chocolate mixture into egg whites.
	\step Pour batter evenly into prepared bowls and bake.
    }
