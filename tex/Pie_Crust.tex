        2/3 cup & Butter\\
        4 - 5 Tbls & Cold Water
    }
    
    \preparation{%
        \step Using a pastry knife or a fork, cut the butter into the flour until only small bits of flour covered butter remain.
        \step Mix in enough water to form a stiff dough.
        \step Divide in half and roll out for your pie.  Cool the filling prior to adding the filling to your crust so as not to melt the crust.
    }
