        1- 8 oz package & Cream Cheese\\
        1 cup & Sugar\\
        1 container & Cool Whip\\
        1 large box & Strawberry Jello\\
        2 cups & Boiling Water\\
        2- 10 oz packages & Frozen Strawberries\\
    }
    
    \preparation{%
        \step Arrange graham crackers on bottom of 9x13 pan.
        \step Combine cream cheese, sugar and Cool Whip and spread over graham crackers.
        \step Mix the jello, water and strawberries and gently pour over the cream cheese layer.
