{Caramel Corn}
    
    \graph
    {% pictures
        small=pic/glass,     % small picture
        big=pic/ingredients  % big picture
    }
    
    \introduction{
        Buttery and sweet and crunchy, and makes more than enough to share.
    }
    
    \ingredients{%
        8 Tbls & Butter \\
        8 Tbls & Margarine\\
        2 cups & Brown Sugar\\
        1/2 cup & Light Corn Syrup\\
        1/2 tsp & Baking Soda\\
        1 tsp & Vanilla\\
        6 Quarts & Popcorn
    }
    
    \preparation{%
        \step Boil butter, margarine, brown sugar and corn syrup for 5 minutes without stirring.
        \step Remove from heat and quickly stir in baking soda and vanilla.  Mix well.
        \step Pour mixture over the popcorn and mix.  Place in the oven for 15 minutes and remix.  Continue for one hour.
        \step At the end of the hour. Turn off the oven and leave the caramel corn in the oven as it cools.
    }
